A revolutionary digital experience designed to transform your visit to Naturhistorisk Museum. Follow the steps below to understand how our solution works and what makes it special.

How Survival Kingdom Works

Our digital solution takes you on a journey through natural history, challenging you to understand survival mechanisms in the animal kingdom. Here's how it works:

1

Download & Connect

Begin your journey by downloading the Survival Kingdom app on your smartphone or tablet. Connect to the museum's WiFi network for the best experience.

The app uses a Futura-inspired design to ensure a seamless and intuitive user interface that matches the museum's visual identity.

Download App Image

2

Scan & Discover

As you explore the museum, scan the QR codes placed next to exhibits to unlock digital content related to each display. Learn fascinating facts about survival strategies in nature.

Scan QR Code Image

3

Complete Challenges

Engage with interactive challenges that test your knowledge and understanding of survival mechanisms. Collect digital badges as you successfully complete each task.

Challenge Image

4

Create Your Survival Profile

Based on your interactions and choices, the app creates a personalized survival profile that highlights which natural adaptation strategies align with your thinking.

Profile Image
