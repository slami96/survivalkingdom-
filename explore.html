Discover Survival Kingdom

A revolutionary digital experience designed to transform your visit to Naturhistorisk Museum. Test your survival knowledge and learn about fascinating animals through an interactive quiz adventure.

How Survival Kingdom Works

Our digital solution takes you on a journey through natural history, challenging you to understand survival mechanisms in the animal kingdom. Here's how it works:

1

Scan QR Code

Begin your adventure by scanning a QR code placed next to an exhibit in the museum. Each QR code launches the Survival Kingdom app and connects you to that specific animal or habitat.

The QR codes are strategically placed throughout the museum to guide your exploration and create a personalized journey.

Choose Your Animal

After scanning the QR code, you'll be presented with a selection of animals related to the exhibit. Choose the animal you want to learn more about and test your survival knowledge against.

4

Take the Survival Quiz

Test your knowledge with a 4-question survival quiz that challenges you to respond correctly to animal encounters. The quiz simulates real-life scenarios where you might encounter the animal in the wild.

Your choices determine your survival success and reveal how well you understand animal behavior in their natural habitats.
