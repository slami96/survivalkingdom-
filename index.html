Exhibitions

Get up close and personal with animals of the world. Visit our exhibitions with more than 2000 preserved animals and make sure to see our special exhibition as well.

Our collections span from prehistoric fossils to modern species, offering insights into the diversity and evolution of life on Earth. Each exhibition is carefully curated to educate and inspire visitors of all ages.

The Mols Laboratory Research Station

The Mols Laboratory is the museum's research station in Mols Bjerge, approx. 40 minutes drive from Aarhus. The scenic landscape and marked trails are perfect for a day trip.

This unique facility allows visitors to see scientific research in action and explore the beautiful natural surroundings. The laboratory focuses on biodiversity research and conservation efforts in the region.
