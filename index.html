Start Your Adventure

Scan the QR code to begin your exploration of the Survival Kingdom App. Learn about fascinating animals and test your survival knowledge!

The QR codes are placed throughout the museum next to key exhibits. Simply open your camera app and point it at any QR code to launch the experience.

A New Way to Experience Natural History

Our innovative digital solution enhances your museum visit by combining educational content with interactive elements, creating a personalized journey through the wonders of natural history. Whether you're a student, a family with children, or simply curious about the world around us, this experience is designed for you.

What Makes Our Digital Solution Special?

The Survival Kingdom App offers visitors an opportunity to engage with exhibits in ways never before possible. Learn about animal behaviors, habitats, and survival mechanisms through interactive challenges and quizzes that put your knowledge to the test.

Developed by Aarhus University Students

This project represents the culmination of research and innovation from dedicated students at Aarhus University, working in collaboration with museum experts to create a truly unique educational tool.
