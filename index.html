Welcome to Naturhistorisk Museum

Discover the wonders of natural history at Aarhus University's Natural History Museum. Our museum offers a journey through time, showcasing the incredible diversity of life on Earth, from prehistoric fossils to modern species.

Immerse yourself in our interactive exhibits, get up close with fascinating specimens, and learn about the delicate balance of ecosystems. Whether you're a science enthusiast, a curious student, or a family looking for an educational adventure, our museum provides an engaging experience for visitors of all ages.

With special exhibitions throughout the year, educational programs, and expert-guided tours, each visit offers something new to discover. Come explore the beauty and complexity of the natural world with us.

Exhibitions

Get up close and personal with animals of the world. Visit our exhibitions with more than 2000 preserved animals and make sure to see our special exhibition as well.

Our collections span from prehistoric fossils to modern species, offering insights into the diversity and evolution of life on Earth. Each exhibition is carefully curated to educate and inspire visitors of all ages.

Kid-friendly Museum

Natural History Museum is for all ages, but children especially. Find out why.

We offer interactive exhibits designed specifically for young explorers, with hands-on activities that make learning about nature fun and engaging. Children can discover the wonders of wildlife through playful experiences tailored to spark curiosity and wonder.

Our dedicated children's area allows kids to touch selected specimens, engage with interactive displays, and participate in regular weekend workshops led by our education team. School holidays feature special activities designed to deepen children's understanding of natural science while keeping them entertained.

Family-friendly tours happen every weekend, focusing on different themes that bring natural history to life for our youngest visitors.

The Mols Laboratory Research Station

The Mols Laboratory is the museum's research station in Mols Bjerge, approx. 40 minutes drive from Aarhus. The scenic landscape and marked trails are perfect for a day trip.

This unique facility allows visitors to see scientific research in action and explore the beautiful natural surroundings. The laboratory focuses on biodiversity research and conservation efforts in the region.
